About Travel Insurance in Indonesia
Travel Insurance was founded in 1890 by name, for 128 years Travel has grown into one of the largest insurance and financial asset management companies in the world.

In its journey, Allianz has become part of the world's social, economic and political dynamics and has been able to go through various gloomy phases that have hit the world, starting from World War I, World War II, and various global economic crises. During that time, Allianz has always been able to maintain its identity, superior performance with innovative products, services and protection solutions.

At the end of 2010, it became the largest asset management company with assets under management of 1,164 billion Euros. In 2014, Travel managed to book revenue of 122.25 billion Euros, with an operating profit of 10.4 billion Euros and attributed its net profit of 6.22 billion Euros to shareholders. Travel Group occupies the position of the 2nd largest insurance company in the world according to Forbes, 2017.
